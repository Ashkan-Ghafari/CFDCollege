About Me

I'm Ashkan Ghafari, a Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering at Concordia University, Montreal, specializing in numerical simulations of Axial Suspension Plasma Spraying using ANSYS Fluent. I have over 10 years of experience in CFD and advanced flow visualization techniques including PIV and shadowgraph. Through CFD College, I share training resources to help students and professionals master CFD tools and techniques.

  • Tecplot: Charting and Analysis of CFD Data
  • MATLAB: Post-processing and Automation Scripts
  • OriginPro: Scientific Graphs and Data Presentation
  • Resume & Scholarship Writing: Academic Career Development

Research Focus

My current research is focused on the numerical modeling of the suspension plasma spraying process using the Mettech Axial III torch. I simulate complex plasma-substrate interactions, optimize torch parameters, and analyze particle dynamics to enhance coating quality and deposition efficiency.
